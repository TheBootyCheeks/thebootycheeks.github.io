Elite Omicron Teams

Gungans (Boss Nass / Phalanx)

Hard Counter GL Leia / GL Ahsoka High volume of hits to strip shield generator stacks.
Efficiency Counter Traya + Nihilus + oSavage Focus ONLY the shield generator until stacks are at 0.
Omicron Counter oGreat Mothers Doom ignores protection, bypassing the shield mechanic.

Jedi (oKelleran Beq / oMace)

Hard Counter GL SLKR / GL JMK Massive damage to break 100% protection recovery.
Efficiency Counter Traya + oSavage Isolate Beq immediately to stop out-of-turn recovery.
Omicron Counter oMalicos (UFU) Burst damage can snipe Beq before 'Second Wind'.

Starkiller (oJuhani / oMara)

Hard Counter GL JML / GL Leia Must absorb Mara's opening Turn Meter burst.
Efficiency Counter Bad Batch (Fast Echo) Echo must be faster than Mara Jade to Daze.
Omicron Counter oTuskens Tuskens gain momentum from every counter-attack.

Phoenix (oCaptain Rex / oHera)

Hard Counter GL SEE / GL SLKR Prevents Rex from building the Turn Meter train.
Efficiency Counter Empire (Vader + oMara) Control Rex immediately or you will never take a turn.
Omicron Counter oTuskens The 'Threads of Destiny' assists feed Tusken stacks.

Queen Amidala (MQJ / POW)

Hard Counter GL Ahsoka / GL JML Reliable bulk to survive the opening swarm.
Efficiency Counter GAS (501st) Reduce max health and focus the Queen to stop assists.
Omicron Counter Bane + oSith Assassin Assassin TM boost is mandatory in TW to beat her speed.

Sep Droids (oDroideka)

Hard Counter GL JMK (Mark DDK) CAT Force Leap ignores DDK's Damage Immunity.
Efficiency Counter Imperial Troopers (Veers) Only if you outspeed. DDK blast will one-shot troopers.
Omicron Counter oRaddus (Rogue One) Raddus 'Hope' wipes the team through any immunities.

Geos (oPoggle / oSpy)

Hard Counter Darth Vader (High Relic) Merciless Massacre must kill Poggle or Spy early.
Efficiency Counter Bad Batch Mass Daze stops the Hive TM loop.
Omicron Counter oTraya / oSavage Geos kill themselves assisting against Traya's unique.

Imp Remnant (oDTMG)

Hard Counter GL Jabba Thermals ignore the high armor of Remnant troopers.
Efficiency Counter GAS (501st) Permanent health reduction is the only way to kill them.
Omicron Counter oMalgus (Sith Empire) Fear helps control the Trooper swarm.

UFU (oMalicos)

Hard Counter GL JML / GL Rey Needs protection to survive Malicos’s initial 100k hit.
Efficiency Counter Imperial Troopers Must kill Malicos before he applies Fear.
Omicron Counter oKelleran Beq Second Wind saves the target of Malicos's burst.

Reva (oGrand Inquisitor)

Hard Counter GL JMK / GL JML Standard Jedi GLs control Purge stacks best.
Efficiency Counter CLS (Rebels) Focus GI first. Requires very high speed mods.
Omicron Counter oCaptain Rex (Phoenix) Rex speed allows Phoenix to out-cycle Inquisitors.

oRaddus (oJyn/oCassian)

Hard Counter GL SEE / GL Jabba Kill them before 'Hope' charges up.
Efficiency Counter Imperial Troopers Focus Raddus or Jyn first to stop the revives.
Omicron Counter oMara Jade (Palp) Stagger and Tenacity down stops the assist train.

oTuskens (Chief/Raider)

Hard Counter GL SLKR / GL SEE Standard AoE and anti-revive handles them easily.
Efficiency Counter Darth Vader (Solo) Dots and Culling Blade handle the health pools.
Omicron Counter oPoggle (Geos) Hive tactics can out-generate Tusken momentum.

oMalgus (Sith Empire)

Hard Counter GL JML / GL SLKR High tenacity is needed to resist 'Fear' spam.
Efficiency Counter Imperial Troopers Requires 380+ speed on Piett. Focus Malgus.
Omicron Counter oCaptain Rex Speed boost allows Phoenix to out-cycle Fear.
