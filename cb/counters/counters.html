TW Elite 20 Counter-Matrix

1. Gungans (Boss Nass / Phalanx)

Hard Counter GL Leia / GL Ahsoka High volume of hits to strip shield generator stacks.
Efficiency Counter Traya + Nihilus + oSavage Focus ONLY the shield generator until stacks are at 0.
Omicron Counter oGreat Mothers (Nighttroopers) Their Doom ignores protection, bypassing the shield mechanic.

2. Jedi (oKelleran Beq / oMace)

Hard Counter GL SLKR / GL JMK Need massive damage to break through 100% protection recovery.
Efficiency Counter Traya + oSavage Isolate Beq immediately. Keeps Mace from taunting.
Omicron Counter oMalicos (UFU) Malicos’s burst damage can snipe Beq before 'Second Wind'.

3. Starkiller (oJuhani / oMara)

Hard Counter GL JML / GL Leia Must absorb Mara's opening Turn Meter burst.
Efficiency Counter Bad Batch (Fast Echo) Echo must be faster than Mara Jade to Daze.
Omicron Counter oTuskens (Chief/Warrior) Tuskens gain momentum from every SK/Juhani counter-attack.

4. Phoenix (oCaptain Rex / oHera)

Hard Counter GL SEE / GL SLKR Prevents Rex from building the Turn Meter train.
Efficiency Counter Empire (Vader + oMara) Control Rex immediately or you will never take a turn.
Omicron Counter oTuskens The 'Threads of Destiny' assists feed Tusken stacks.

5. Queen Amidala (MQJ / POW)

Hard Counter GL Ahsoka / GL JML Reliable bulk to survive the opening handmaiden swarm.
Efficiency Counter GAS (501st) Reduce max health and focus the Queen to stop assists.
Omicron Counter Bane + oSith Assassin In TW, Assassin provides the TM boost needed for Bane to move.

6. Sep Droids (oDroideka)

Hard Counter GL JMK (Mark DDK) CAT Force Leap ignores DDK's Damage Immunity.
Efficiency Counter Imperial Troopers (Veers) Only if you outspeed. DDK blast will one-shot troopers.
Omicron Counter oRaddus (Rogue One) Raddus 'Hope' wipes the team through any immunities.

7. Geos (oPoggle / oSpy)

Hard Counter Darth Vader (High Relic) Merciless Massacre must kill Poggle or Spy early.
Efficiency Counter Bad Batch Mass Daze stops the Hive TM loop. High success rate.
Omicron Counter oTraya / oSavage Geos kill themselves by assisting against Traya.

8. Imp Remnant (oDTMG)

Hard Counter GL Jabba Thermals ignore the high armor of Remnant troopers.
Efficiency Counter GAS (501st) Permanent health reduction is the only way to kill them.
Omicron Counter oMalgus (Sith Empire) Malgus can Fear the swarm to prevent mass assists.

9. UFU (oMalicos)

Hard Counter GL JML / GL Rey Needs protection to survive Malicos’s initial 100k+ hit.
Efficiency Counter Imperial Troopers (Fast Piett) Kill Malicos before he takes his second turn.
Omicron Counter oKelleran Beq Second Wind saves the target of Malicos's Special 2.

10. Nightsisters (oGreat Mothers)

Hard Counter GL Ahsoka / GL JMK Blessing/Mastery helps bypass the 'Doom' mechanic.
Efficiency Counter Jabba the Hutt Jabba is immune to many of the Mothers' debuffs.
Omicron Counter oDTMG Remnant bulk can survive until an opening appears.

11. GL Leia Organa (AdRad/Drogan)

Hard Counter GL Leia (Mirror) / GL Jabba Jabba is the most consistent non-mirror win.
Efficiency Counter JML + JCK Jedi Cal's instakill can remove Drogan early.
Omicron Counter Bane + oSith Assassin Can work if Bane is Relic 9 and Assassin outspeeds Drogan.

12. GL Lord Vader (Maul)

Hard Counter GL JMK (with CAT) Force Leap Maul immediately to stop the 5-stack frenzy.
Efficiency Counter Fennec Shand (Bounty Hunters) Mando Disintegrate on Maul is mandatory. High risk.
Omicron Counter oBo-Katan (Mandos) TW Omicron allows Bo-Katan to shred LV's stacks.

13. GL JML (JKR/Bastila)

Hard Counter GL SEE / GL Leia SEE is the ultimate JML killer due to deception.
Efficiency Counter Darth Revan (Sith Empire) Fear and Deathmark can bypass JML's taunt.
Omicron Counter oSith Trio (Traya) Traya Isolate on JML prevents him from sharing Master's Training.

14. GL Jabba (Boushh/Krrsantan)

Hard Counter GL JMK / GL SLKR High damage burst required to kill Krrsantan twice.
Efficiency Counter Aphra (with Droids) Siphon and turn meter manipulation shuts down Jabba.
Omicron Counter oDTMG Dark Trooper Gideon provides the punch needed to break Jabba.

15. Reva (oGrand Inquisitor)

Hard Counter GL JMK / GL JML Standard Jedi GLs control the Purge stacks best.
Efficiency Counter CLS (Rebels) All-in on Grand Inquisitor first. Very high mod requirement.
Omicron Counter oCaptain Rex (Phoenix) Rex allows Phoenix to out-speed and daze the Inquisitors.

16. GL Rey (oBen Solo)

Hard Counter GL SEE / GL JMK SEE with Wat is the safest way to bypass the 'Lifeblood' tanking.
Efficiency Counter SK (Palp/Mara) Starkiller can shock Ben Solo to prevent the revive.
Omicron Counter oMalicos Can one-shot Rey if she is below 50% health.

17. oRaddus (oJyn/oCassian)

Hard Counter GL SEE / GL Jabba Must kill them before the 'Hope' ultimate charges up.
Efficiency Counter Imperial Troopers Classic TM train; focus Raddus or Jyn first.
Omicron Counter oMara Jade (Palp) Stagger and Tenacity down stops the Rogue One assists.

18. oMalgus (Sith Empire)

Hard Counter GL JML / GL SLKR High tenacity/Mastery is needed to resist the 'Fear' spam.
Efficiency Counter Imperial Troopers Can work if Piett is 380+ speed. Focus Malgus.
Omicron Counter oCaptain Rex (Phoenix) Rex’s speed boost can allow Phoenix to out-cycle Malgus.

19. oTuskens (Chief/Raider)

Hard Counter GL SLKR / GL SEE Standard AoE and anti-revive mechanics handle Tuskens easily.
Efficiency Counter Darth Vader (Solo) Dots and Culling Blade handle the high health pools.
Omicron Counter oPoggle (Geos) Hive tactics can out-generate the Tusken momentum.

20. Bad Batch (Hunter/Echo)

Hard Counter GL JML / GL SEE Requires high tenacity or pre-taunts to stop the daze.
Efficiency Counter Imperial Troopers The winner is whoever’s Echo or Piett moves first.
Omicron Counter oMara Jade Mara's 100% TM ensures she moves first in TW.
